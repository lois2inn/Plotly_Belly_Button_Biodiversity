Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

The Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard enables researchers and research participants access identified bacterial species found inside the belly button of humans. The interactive charts help visualize the different bacteria collected from the research participants. The analysis can be started by using the drop-down menu to select a participant's ID. Upon doing so, the bar chart will be updated to display the top 10 bacteria cultures for that individual. The gauge chart shows the washing frequency giving the number of scrubs per week. The size of bubbles in the bubble chart shows the sample's concentration and biodiversity of the bacteria.
